Coffee is a ritual, a habit, and for many people, a quiet moment before the world wakes up.

Learn about coffee

What is coffee?

Coffee is made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries grown on coffee plants. Once brewed, it produces a rich, aromatic drink enjoyed across cultures and continents.

Popular coffee styles

Espresso

A concentrated shot of coffee with a bold flavor and a layer of crema on top.

Latte

Espresso mixed with steamed milk, creating a smooth and creamy texture.

Cold Brew

Coffee brewed slowly in cold water, resulting in a less acidic and refreshing drink.

How coffee is brewed

Brewing coffee involves extracting flavor from ground beans using water. The grind size, temperature, and time all affect the final taste.

From quick espresso shots to slow pour-overs, each method creates a different experience.
